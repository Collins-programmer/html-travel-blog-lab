Adventures in Fire Island

One of the biggest attractions is the miles of clean undeveloped beach along Antlantic Ocean. The park has several boardwalks and trails that go through dunes and forests.I also various wildlife like deers and butterflies.

Key features of the park include.

  1. Sand dunes
  2. Maritime forests
  3. Historic sites
  4. Car-free environment

Explore the adventure
